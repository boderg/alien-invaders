Mission Brief:


Humanity and Earth itself is at risk!

Alien forces are gathering to conquer and plunder Earths's already fragile ecosystem and enslave any remaining humans on the planet.

As part of the Earth Defence League, you are tasked with the protection of all life and resources against the Alien Invaders.

It is your job to destroy all alien ships that jump into Earth's space and rid our galaxy of the invading forces.

Man Up soldier and Fight for your survival!



How to Play!


The game is timed at 30 secs.

Alien ships appear at random and disappear after a pre-determined time period.

It is your mission to target as many of these alien craft as possible.

Use your mouse to target the enemy in your crosshairs.
Fire using left click on your mouse.

Sometimes a friendly defence craft may stray into your firing zone.
DO NOT attack it or you will lose points!

Points are awarded for each ship destroyed as follows:

  • 5 Points for each red alien enemy
  • 10 Points for each blue alien enemy
  • 15 Points for each green alien enemy
  • 15 Points deducted for each friendly shot down


Be alert, stay awake and fight the Alien Invaders!
